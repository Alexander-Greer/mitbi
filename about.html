WHO WE ARE

Make It to Break It was founded by a group of MIT students in March of 2022 with the goal of building things to break world records!

Our current project is to break the record for the world's largest Rube Goldberg machine with a goal of more than 500 actions! Crazy!

We comprise about 50 students from MIT and beyond, all of whom are dedicated to using their creative and technical skills to create things the world has never seen.
